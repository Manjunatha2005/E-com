Why Button Mushroom?

It cooks fast, soaks up flavour well, and works in soups, stir-fries, grills, pasta, and breakfast dishes. If you want a simple mushroom that fits most recipes, this is the one to start with.

Cooking notes

Use a hot pan, a little fat, and avoid overcrowding so the mushrooms brown instead of steaming.
