Product Information

Benefits

Enjoy the rich, earthy taste of Cremini Mushrooms, also known as Baby Bellas or Baby Portobellos. These mushrooms offer a deeper umami flavor and firmer texture than white button mushrooms, making them a versatile choice for a variety of dishes.

Features

Cremini mushrooms are ideal for high-heat cooking, and their savory, full-bodied taste pairs beautifully with meats, vegetables, and grains.

Storage Tip

Referigerate it

Disclaimer

Store mushrooms in a cool, dry environment. For longer-lasting freshness, keep fresh mushrooms in a paper bag in the refrigerator. To enjoy them at their best, consume mushrooms within a few days of purchase. Steer clear of any mushrooms that appear slimy, discolored, or have a strong smell.

How to use

Cooking Tips

Clean gently with a damp paper towel. Sauté in butter or olive oil until golden brown for a rich, savory dish. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs to enhance flavor.

Serve Suggestions

Add to stir-fries, top pizzas, or toss in salads. For a gourmet touch, sauté with butter, thyme, and a splash of white wine.

Where to Use

  • Stir-fries and noodle bowls
  • Pastas, risottos, and grain bowls
  • Pizza toppings and baked dishes
  • Soups, sauces, and creamy skillet meals
