Description

Grown on natural oak logs in temperature-controlled growing rooms, our Shiitake mushrooms develop their signature meaty texture and deep, savoury flavour. Each pack is harvested at peak maturity and packed within 24 hours of picking to ensure maximum freshness.

Shiitake are one of the most nutritionally dense mushrooms available. A single 100g serving provides 40% of your daily copper needs, significant B6 and B5 (pantothenic acid), plus lentinan — a powerful polysaccharide known for immune modulation. They are also one of the rare plant sources of vitamin D when sun-dried.
